## Permission for reuse
Whether you can reuse these assets in your own Stardew Valley mod depends on the author listed under [_credits_](#credits):

author | usage guidelines
------ | ----------------
_unknown_ | ❌ You **can not** use these (unless we track down who contributed them).
Yoseiri   | ❌ You **can not** use these due to restrictive permissions.
[ArtofTic](https://artoftic.itch.io/pixelart-16x16-fooddrinks)<br />[Cyangmou](https://cyangmou.itch.io/pixel-food-cooking-icons-16x16)<br />[Kazzter](https://kazzter-k.itch.io/food-fruits-and-veggies)<br />[Pixeltiers](https://pixeltier.itch.io/pixeltiers-food-rpg-icon-set) | ⚠ These are paid asset packs. You **must purchase your own license** to use these. If you use versions marked as modified below, you also need to credit the author who modified it.
all others | ✔ You _can_ use these if fully credited, and it's clear which author contributed each sprite. (For example, don't just say "this mod has images from MizuJakkaru and ParadigmNomad" and leave it to the reader to figure out which sprites are from who.)

Note that these are **not open-source**. For reuse in another context (like non-mods or mods for another game), please contact the author.
